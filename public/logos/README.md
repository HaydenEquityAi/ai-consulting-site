## Logo Specifications:

- **Format**: PNG (with transparency preferred)
- **Size**: Recommended 48x48px to 96x96px (will be scaled to 48x48px in UI)
- **Background**: Transparent or white
- **Aspect Ratio**: Square (1:1)
